Frequently Asked Questions

  • What do people want in education?

    People generally seek personalized learning experiences, engaging content, practical applications, and flexible learning options. They also value competent instructors, resources for skill development, and career-oriented programs.

  • How can I pay for my education?

    Payment methods may include credit or debit cards, bank transfers, digital wallets (e.g., PayPal), or other online payment platforms. Additionally, some educational institutions offer payment plans, scholarships, or financial aid options.

  • What are the benefits of online learning?

    Online learning offers flexibility, allowing students to learn at their own pace and on their schedule. It often provides access to a wide range of courses and resources, as well as the opportunity to connect with instructors and peers from around the world.

Tutoring Services Policy

Close

1. Introduction:

Our tutoring services aim to provide academic support and guidance to students in various subjects and topics. The following policy outlines the terms and conditions that govern our tutoring sessions.

2. Scope of Services:

We offer tutoring sessions in subjects such as Math, Science, English, History, and more. Tutoring can be conducted in-person or online, based on the preference of the student and tutor availability. Sessions may cover homework help, exam preparation, concept clarification, and skill development.

3. Scheduling:

Students can schedule tutoring sessions based on availability and subject to tutor availability. Sessions are typically scheduled in advance to ensure adequate preparation time for both the student and the tutor. Any changes or cancellations to scheduled sessions must be communicated at least 24 hours in advance.

4. Fees and Payment:

Tutoring fees are determined based on the duration of the session and the subject matter. Payment is typically due at the time of booking or before the start of the session. We accept various payment methods, including cash, credit/debit cards, and online payment platforms.

5. Conduct and Expectations:

Both tutors and students are expected to conduct themselves professionally and respectfully during tutoring sessions. Tutors will provide personalized support and guidance tailored to the individual needs and learning styles of the students. Students are encouraged to actively participate in the sessions, ask questions, and communicate any challenges they may be facing.

6. Academic Integrity:

We uphold the principles of academic integrity and do not condone any form of cheating or plagiarism. Tutors will assist students in understanding concepts and completing assignments but will not provide solutions or assistance that violates academic integrity policies.

7. Feedback and Improvement:

We welcome feedback from students regarding their tutoring experience and strive to continually improve our services. Students are encouraged to provide constructive feedback to help us better meet their academic needs and goals.

8. Confidentiality:

We respect the privacy and confidentiality of our students and their academic information. All tutoring sessions and communications will be kept confidential unless otherwise required by law or agreed upon by the student.

9. Cancellation and Refund Policy:

Students may cancel a tutoring session without penalty if done at least 24 hours in advance. No-shows or cancellations made less than 24 hours before the scheduled session may be subject to a cancellation fee. Refunds may be provided in certain circumstances, such as if the tutor cancels the session or if there are technical issues preventing the session from taking place.

10. Contact Information:

For inquiries, scheduling, or any concerns regarding tutoring services, please contact us at info.learniq@gmail.com.
